KAMIEN I CO?

Pictures of a tenement house, which survived the World War II. It is located on Walicow Street, which used to be a part of a Jewish ghetto in Warsaw. The mural on the ruined side of the building translates as "A rock, and so what?" (Kamien = a rock, but also a tenement house) which playlfully challenges the viewer's historical knowledge on the place.

This work was exhibited in April 2016 at 89th Annual McLean County Amateur Art Exhibition in Brandt Gallery in Bloomington, Illinois. It has also won 2016 McLean County Art Center 1st Place Award in Photography.
